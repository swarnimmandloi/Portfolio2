Reimagining

Customer

Support for

Azure

Azure, Microsoft's cloud computing platform, serves a vast user base including many of the world's top 500 companies. These enterprises rely on Azure for critical services, requiring 24/7 support to ensure business continuity. As a key revenue generator for Microsoft, the Azure support system plays a crucial role in maintaining customer satisfaction and loyalty.

My Role

Led the redesign of Azure's customer support experience, focusing on empathy-driven improvements to enhance customer satisfaction and optimize support processes.

Get in touch for more details

Understanding
the Problem

Despite its importance, the Azure support system faced several challenges:

Customer dissatisfaction evident from online reviews

High volume of support tickets, each incurring operational costs

Opportunities for process optimization and efficiency improvements

Need for enhanced customer satisfaction and reduced ticket resolution times

Research and
Analysis

Our team conducted extensive research to understand the complex support ecosystem:

Analyzed different support case types and their varying urgency levels

Identified the first email contact as a critical touchpoint for customer satisfaction

Leveraged existing research insights and conducted new studies

Performed competitive analysis with Google Cloud and AWS support systems

Examined the end-to-end ticket creation and resolution process

Key Areas for
Improvement

Based on our research, we identified several areas for enhancement:

First contact email redesign

Video content creation and improvement

Streamlining the ticket creation and resolution process

Addressing frequently recurring tickets

Design Process

For each area of improvement, we followed a rigorous design process:

Created multiple iterations (V1, V2, V3) for email and video content

Conducted design background checks to ensure alignment with Azure brand guidelines

Iterated based on feedback and A/B testing results

Data-Driven
Approach

We analyzed ticket data to identify:

Frequently recurring issues suitable for video content creation

Patterns in ticket creation and resolution to inform process improvements

Stakeholder
Engagement

Presented improvement ideas to the worldwide leader of Microsoft Support

Initiated discussions for involving Internal Developer Community (IDC) in the improvement process

Outcomes and
Next Steps

Redesigned first contact email to improve customer experience

Developed a strategy for creating targeted video content for common issues

Proposed enhancements for the Azure portal ticket creation and resolution process

Gained leadership buy-in for implementing proposed changes

Ongoing collaboration with IDC to implement technical improvements
